01. About Me

Rooted in curiosity, driven by purpose

I'm Gauri Maheshwari, a Biology-Chemistry pre-majors student in my 2nd year of the Integrated BS-MS programme at IISER Mohali. My journey began with a fascination for understanding how molecules shape life — from the quantum dance of electrons in a spectroscopic measurement to the elegant complexity of ecological networks.

I thrive on learning new techniques, whether it's analyzing plant functional traits in the Western Ghats or exploring the principles of satellite interferometry.
